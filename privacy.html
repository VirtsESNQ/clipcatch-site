Privacy policy

The short version. ClipCatch records your screen locally on your Mac. Your recordings never leave your computer unless you choose to share them. We don't have a server that sees your video.

What ClipCatch records

The app captures the last 60 seconds of your screen (and optionally microphone input) into a rolling buffer in memory. When you press the save hotkey, that buffer is written to a video file on your local disk in the folder you selected. Nothing is uploaded.

Data we receive

  • License activation. When you enter a license key, the app sends the key to Polar.sh to verify and activate it. Polar receives the key, your machine identifier, and basic activation metadata.
  • Checkout. When you purchase, payment is processed by Polar.sh and their payment partners. We never see card details.
  • Support email. If you email support@clipcatch.app, your email and message are received and stored in our inbox.
  • Website visits. The site is hosted on Netlify, which receives standard server logs (IP address, user-agent, timestamp). We do not run any third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

What we do not collect

  • We do not upload, store, or analyze your recordings.
  • We do not collect telemetry from the app about what you record.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.

Sub-processors

  • Polar.sh — payments, licensing, downloads (privacy policy).
  • Netlify — website hosting and DNS (privacy policy).
  • ImprovMX + Google — email forwarding and the support@ mailbox.

Your rights

If you're in the EU/UK, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and portability. To exercise any of them, email support@clipcatch.app and we'll respond within 30 days.
