What makes it different

Two features that matter for tape.

Area monitor

A watchdog pointed at your newest bar.

Drag a rectangle around the latest candle. When the pixels inside change more than your threshold, ClipCatch auto-saves the 60 seconds leading up to it. No manual saves, no missed moves.

Save-before

The clip of what you just watched.

You press the button after the move. ClipCatch rewinds into RAM, writes the previous 60 seconds to disk, and keeps recording. Save 30 clips in a session without thinking.

Answers

Trader-specific questions.

Can I use it for my funded eval?

Yes. Export as MP4 with a timestamp overlay. Works as legal evidence for every major eval firm we've tested.

Does my mentor group see the clip?

Only if you share it. Clips are written to your local Movies folder. Nothing uploads, nothing syncs.

Can I record two charts at once?

Yes — run multiple area monitors, each with its own region. They share one buffer engine to keep CPU low.

Does it survive a platform restart?

ClipCatch tracks the window, not the process. Restart NinjaTrader, MotiveWave, whatever — ClipCatch reacquires when the window comes back.

What about crypto exchanges?

Same deal. If it's on your screen — Bybit, Bitget, TradingView on BTCUSD — we record it.

I trade on my iPad. Help?

You'd need an iPad on your Mac (Sidecar or mirroring). Once it's on the desktop, ClipCatch captures it like any other source.
